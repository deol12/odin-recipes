Tira Misu Recipe


Ingredients

  • Lady Fingers
  • Mascarpone
  • Espresso
  • Heavy Whipped Cream
  • Granulated Sugar
  • Vanilla Extract
  • Cocoa Powder

  1. Mix creamy filling. - Beat the mascarpone, cream, sugar, and vanilla together until stiff peaks.
  2. Dip lady fingers. Add the espresso and liqueur (if using) to a shallow bowl and dip the lady fingers on boths sides (don't let them soak-just a quick dip!)
  3. Layer mascarpone. Smooth a layer of the mascarpone/whipped cream mixture on top of the lady fingers.
  4. Repeat.
